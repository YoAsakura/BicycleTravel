Bicycle FAQs: Bike for Sale and Prices in the Philippines

Is it more profitable to buy or assemble yourself? In almost all variants, a self-assembled bike will be more profitable than a purchased one. Rule of thumb: if your budget is less than $ 1,500 (~ ₱ 75,000) or even $ 1,700 (~ ₱ 93,000), it is much more profitable to buy a pre-assembled bike, especially if it is last year's model.

How much does a mountain bike cost?

Mountain bike prices in the Philippines range from ₱ 18,000. The prices for new generations of mountain bikes in the Philippines can be as high as: 1,200,000 и, and although innovative technology has been used for these bikes, there is no particular need to purchase such a bike, you can still enjoy riding on a cheaper version.
